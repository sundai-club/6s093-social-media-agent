# Emanon - Business Overview

**Tagline:** NO HYPE, HIGH ALPHA

Emanon is an AI news, consulting, and education platform focused on delivering practical, non-hype-driven AI insights for builders and business leaders.

## Mission

Deliver curated, actionable intelligence about AI developments without marketing fluff. Emanon cuts through the noise to provide real value for people implementing AI in their work.

## Core Offerings

1. **Weekly AI Newsletter & Podcast** - Curated AI news delivered every Sunday
2. **AI Strategy Consulting** - 1-hour sessions for implementation planning
3. **Technical Blog & Guides** - In-depth articles on deploying LLMs and AI tools
4. **Community Platform** - Discord community for AI practitioners

## Key Differentiators

- **No hype approach** - Focuses on practical, actionable insights
- **Multi-language support** - Content available in 11+ languages
- **Builder-focused** - Targets people actually implementing AI, not just reading about it
- **Unbiased analysis** - Tool comparisons and benchmarks without vendor influence

# Emanon - Business Model

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Recurring Subscriptions (Stripe Billing)

Three-tier subscription model:

| Tier | Price | Benefits |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Reader** | $5/month | Name on supporters wall, priority topic requests |
| **Contributor** | $15/month | Discord role, behind-the-scenes audio drops, early access to tooling |
| **Architect** | $50/month | Quarterly strategy huddle, private office hours, Emanon swag |

### 2. One-time Donations

- Flexible donation amounts ($1 - $10,000 USD)
- Processed through Stripe
- Supports content creation and platform development

### 3. Consulting Services

- 1-hour AI strategy sessions
- Booked through Calendly
- Focus on implementation planning and tool selection

### 4. Partnership Opportunities

- Marketing collaborations
- Co-branded initiatives
- Contact: contact@emanon.dev

## Cost Structure

**Content Production:**
- Newsletter curation and writing
- Podcast production
- Blog article creation
- Translation services (automated via Claude API)

**Technology:**
- Cloudflare hosting and workers
- Stripe payment processing
- Resend email delivery
- Anthropic API usage

**Community:**
- Discord server management
- Community engagement

---

## Growth Strategy

1. **Content-led growth** - Weekly newsletter builds audience organically
2. **Multi-language expansion** - 11+ language support for global reach
3. **Community building** - Discord creates retention and engagement
4. **Consulting upsells** - Free content converts to paid consulting
5. **Tiered subscriptions** - Multiple price points capture different segments

- Discord community size and engagement
- Content consumption (page views, podcast listens)
